Why We Built This

Communication is a fundamental human right, yet millions of deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face barriers every single day.

My name is Dhaerya Khanna, and I created this project with a simple goal: to use technology to dismantle those barriers. By translating spoken words into visual Sign Language in real-time, we hope to create a more inclusive society.
